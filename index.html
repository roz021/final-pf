Hi I'm Roy.

I'm currently an undergraduate student from the University of California, San Diego majoring in human computer interaction and minoring in computer science.

As a designer, my focus lies in user experience and visual design so that users can have an immersive and unobtrusive experience. I believe that a good product must have an even better design to back it up. I work passionately with all my projects, and love to push my limits to see what creative ideas I can come up with.

Currently I am the lead designer at Exalton, and also do side work for various start ups and organizations on campus.

Other things I enjoy doing include playing RPGs like Zelda or Assassin's Creed and scouring the neighborhood on Pokemon Go. I'm also an intense cat dad, but dogs are cool too :).
